### Role Summary
The Head of Marketing & Communications will be responsible for storytelling and brand representation for garden3d across global digital platforms. As part of a worker-owned creative collective, this role focuses on communicating the mission of a decentralized organization that spans branding, IoT, and cross-platform applications.

### Required Skills
*   **Strategic Storytelling:** Ability to craft and execute a cohesive brand narrative across various digital channels.
*   **Digital Marketing:** Expertise in managing online presence and communicating complex creative/tech concepts to a global audience.
*   **Cross-Platform Communication:** Proficiency in navigating diverse media landscapes to represent a multi-faceted collective (including Sanctuary Computer, XXIX, and Index Space).

### Qualifications
*   **Experience in Creative/Tech Ecosystems:** Proven track record in marketing for design studios, software teams, or creative collectives.
*   **Global Mindset:** Ability to work effectively within a decentralized, remote, and global community structure.
*   **Alignment with Collective Values:** A strong commitment to worker-ownership models, transparency, and shared responsibility.

### Core Responsibilities
*   **Brand Narrative Management:** Telling the "garden3d story" across the internet to engage a global audience.
*   **Multi-Entity Representation:** Communicating the value and mission of the various arms of the collective (Development, Design, and Community).
*   **Community Engagement:** Promoting the collective's mission of innovation, open-source principles, and creative collaboration.
